## English

### 1. Who we are

"团团" (TuanTuan, hereafter "the Platform") is an order-aggregation platform connecting small food merchants with local consumers (e.g., students within a campus or residential community).

- **Operator**: TuanTuan Operator (individual operator, not a registered company)
- **Contact**: nihaotuantuan@gmail.com
- **WhatsApp**: 013-283 1238

### 2. Personal data we collect

When you use the Platform as a customer, we collect:

| Data | When collected | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Name | First profile setup | To address you and on receipts |
| Mobile phone number | First profile setup | Order identification & merchant contact |
| Delivery address (building + room/marker) | First profile setup | Routing the order |
| Payment screenshot | Each order checkout | Proof of payment for merchant verification |
| Order history | Automatically on each order | Tracking & recurring purchase |
| Web Push subscription endpoint | When you allow notifications | Sending order status notifications |
| Device user-agent (browser type) | When subscribing to push | Debugging notification delivery issues |

We **do NOT** collect: payment card numbers, MyKad, IP address tracking, location GPS, age/gender, social media login info, biometrics, or browsing history outside this Platform.

### 3. Where your data is stored

- **Google Sheets** (operated by Google LLC, servers globally) — for orders, customer info, merchant data
- **Google Drive** (operated by Google LLC) — for payment screenshots
- **Cloudflare Workers** (operated by Cloudflare Inc., servers globally) — for push notification routing and read-cache, *not* permanent storage
- **Your browser** (localStorage) — locally cached profile and order list for offline access

Data may be transferred outside Malaysia to data centers operated by Google and Cloudflare. By using the Platform, you consent to such cross-border transfer.

### 4. Who can access your data

- **You** — full access via the Platform UI
- **Your merchant** (the restaurant you ordered from) — sees your name, phone, address, items, payment screenshot, for delivery purpose only
- **The Platform Operator** (TuanTuan Operator) — for technical support and dispute resolution
- **NOT shared with**: advertisers, third-party analytics, government (unless legally compelled), or any unrelated party

### 5. How long we keep your data

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Customer profile (name/phone/address) | Until you request deletion |
| Order records | 12 months after order completion, then archived |
| Payment screenshots | 6 months, then deleted |
| Web Push subscription | Until you unsubscribe or fail to receive 5 consecutive pushes |
| System logs | 30 days rolling |

### 6. Your rights under PDPA

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data — email us, we'll send within 21 days
- **Correct** inaccurate data — edit in-app or email us
- **Delete** your data — email us with the subject "Delete My Data" + your phone number
- **Withdraw consent** — disable notifications in browser, stop using the Platform
- **Complain to PDP Commissioner** if you believe we mishandled your data — [pdp.gov.my](https://www.pdp.gov.my/)

### 7. Cookies and tracking

We do **not** use any third-party tracking (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).
We use **browser localStorage** only — to remember your profile so you don't re-enter it next time. This is local to your device only and never sent to any server we don't operate.

### 8. Children

The Platform is intended for users aged 18 or above (university students and adults). If you are under 18, please use the Platform with parental consent.

### 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Material changes will be notified via in-app banner. Continued use after notification = consent to updated policy.

### 10. Contact

For any privacy concerns: nihaotuantuan@gmail.com

## 中文（Bahasa Cina）

### 1. 我们是谁

"团团" 是一个连接本地小型餐饮商家与社区消费者（如校园学生或宿舍居民）的订单聚合平台。

- **运营者**：团团运营者（个人运营，未注册公司）
- **联系邮箱**：nihaotuantuan@gmail.com
- **WhatsApp**：013-283 1238

### 2. 我们收集什么个人资料

当你作为客户使用本平台时，我们收集：

| 资料 | 收集时机 | 用途 |
|---|---|---|
| 姓名 | 首次填写资料时 | 称呼你 + 显示在收据上 |
| 手机号 | 首次填写资料时 | 订单识别 + 商家联系 |
| 配送地址（楼栋 + 房号/标记）| 首次填写资料时 | 配送路由 |
| 支付截图 | 每次下单时 | 给商家核对付款 |
| 订单历史 | 每次下单自动记录 | 状态追踪 + 复购 |
| Web Push 订阅 endpoint | 当你允许通知时 | 发送订单状态通知 |
| 浏览器类型（user-agent）| 订阅推送时 | 排查通知送达问题 |

我们**不收集**：信用卡号、MyKad、IP 追踪、GPS 位置、年龄/性别、社交账号、生物特征、平台外浏览历史。

### 3. 你的数据存在哪里

- **Google Sheets**（Google LLC 运营，全球服务器）—— 订单、客户信息、商家数据
- **Google Drive**（Google LLC 运营）—— 支付截图
- **Cloudflare Workers**（Cloudflare Inc. 运营，全球服务器）—— 推送通知中转 + 读缓存，**不**做永久存储
- **你的浏览器 localStorage**—— 本地缓存的资料和订单列表，方便离线访问

数据可能传输至马来西亚境外的 Google 和 Cloudflare 数据中心。**使用本平台即表示你同意此类跨境传输**。

### 4. 谁能看到你的数据

- **你自己** — 在平台 UI 中完全可见
- **你下单的商家** — 仅看到姓名、电话、地址、菜品、支付截图，**仅供配送**
- **平台运营者（团团运营者）** — 用于技术支持和纠纷处理
- **不会共享给**：广告商、第三方分析工具、政府部门（除非法律强制要求）、任何不相关的第三方

### 5. 数据保留多久

| 资料 | 保留时长 |
|---|---|
| 客户资料（姓名/电话/地址）| 直到你要求删除 |
| 订单记录 | 完成后 12 个月，之后归档 |
| 支付截图 | 6 个月后删除 |
| Web Push 订阅 | 直到你取消订阅或连续 5 次推送失败 |
| 系统日志 | 滚动 30 天 |

### 6. 你的 PDPA 权利

你有权：
- **查看**你的个人资料 —— 邮件给我们，21 天内回复
- **更正**不准确的资料 —— app 内编辑或邮件给我们
- **删除**你的数据 —— 邮件主题写「删除我的资料」+ 手机号
- **撤回同意** —— 浏览器关通知、停止使用本平台
- 如认为我们处理不当，可向 **PDP 委员** 投诉：[pdp.gov.my](https://www.pdp.gov.my/)

### 7. Cookies 和追踪

我们**不使用**任何第三方追踪（Google Analytics、Facebook Pixel 等）。
仅使用浏览器 **localStorage** 记住你的资料避免重复填写。这是你设备本地的，不会上传到我们不运营的服务器。

### 8. 未成年人

本平台面向 **18 岁以上**用户（大学生和成年人）。如果你未满 18 岁，请在父母同意下使用。

### 9. 政策变更

我们可能更新本政策。顶部「最后更新」日期反映变更。重大变更将通过 app 内横幅通知。**收到通知后继续使用即视为同意更新后的政策**。

### 10. 联系方式

任何隐私问题：nihaotuantuan@gmail.com
